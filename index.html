About Me

A word I live by

Hello! I’m Ashley — a junior at USC majoring in Data Science and East Asian Languages and Cultures. I’m especially drawn to machine learning and data analytics, where I get to explore patterns hidden in data and uncover meaningful insights.

Beyond tech, I’ve always had a deep love for the Japanese language and culture — from its rich traditions to the exciting innovations in robotics. I also enjoy web development because it gives me a creative space to bring ideas to life and express who I am.

My projects reflect this mix of interests: you’ll find everything from ML projects and hand-coded websites to research on Japanese trends and cultural studies. I hope you enjoy exploring them under the Projects tab! You can also find my Japanese blog linked in the Contact Me section.
